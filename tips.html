Do's and Don'ts of Cyber Safety

✅ Do's

  • Use strong, unique passwords.
  • Enable two-factor authentication.
  • Keep your software up to date.
  • Use secure and trusted websites only.
  • Backup your important files regularly.

❌ Don'ts

  • Don't share personal information online.
  • Don't use public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions.
  • Don't click on unknown links or ads.
  • Don't reuse passwords across sites.
  • Don't download unknown attachments or files.
