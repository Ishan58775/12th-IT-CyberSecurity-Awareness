Types of Cyber Threats

1. Phishing

Phishing is a type of attack where fake emails or websites try to trick you into revealing personal information like passwords or banking details.

2. Malware

Malware stands for malicious software such as viruses, worms, and Trojans that damage or steal data from your computer.

3. Ransomware

This software locks your files and demands money (a ransom) to unlock them.

4. Denial of Service (DoS)

This attack floods a website or network with traffic, causing it to crash and become unusable.
